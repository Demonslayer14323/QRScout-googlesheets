QRScout to Google Sheets Integrator

How to use:

  1. Use QRScout at https://frc2713.github.io/QRScout/ to collect scouting data
  2. When you commit the data, click "Copy Data" in the QR modal
  3. Paste the copied data into the box below
  4. Click "Send to Google Sheets" to append the data to your sheet
