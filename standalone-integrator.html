QRScout Standalone Integrator

How to use:

  1. Use QRScout to collect scouting data
  2. Copy the data from the QR modal
  3. Paste the data into the box below
  4. Ensure the Apps Script URL is correct (update if redeployed)
  5. Click "Send to Google Sheets"
